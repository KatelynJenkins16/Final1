Buffalo Chicken Sandwhich

    Ingredients:
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (or thighs)
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch (optional, for extra crispiness)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons each: garlic powder, onion powder, paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2–3 tablespoons Buffalo wing sauce (like Frank’s RedHot)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1–2 tablespoons milk (any kind)
  • 4 hamburger or brioche buns
    Instructions:
  1. Pound chicken breasts to even thickness and pat dry.
  2. Mix flour, cornstarch, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together egg, milk, and 2 tablespoons Buffalo sauce.
  4. Coat chicken in the flour mixture, then dip into egg mixture, then coat again in flour mixture. Let rest 5 minutes.
  5. Cook chicken by baking at 425°F (220°C) for 15–20 minutes (flip halfway), or pan-fry 3–4 minutes per side, or deep-fry at 350–375°F (175–190°C) for 8–10 minutes until cooked through.
  6. Toss cooked chicken with extra Buffalo sauce.
  7. Toast buns if desired, then assemble sandwiches with chicken and your favorite toppings.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Pesto Chicken Sandwhich

    Instructions:
  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and Italian seasoning.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat. Cook the chicken for about 4–7 minutes per side, until fully cooked and browned.
  3. Place mozzarella slices on the chicken during the last minute of cooking to melt the cheese.
  4. Lightly brush the sandwich rolls with olive oil and toast them on the grill or in a pan until golden.
  5. Spread pesto generously on the inside of the top buns.
  6. Assemble the sandwich by placing the cooked chicken with melted mozzarella on the bottom bun, then add tomato slices and arugula or spinach.
  7. Top with the pesto-spread bun and serve immediately.
    Ingredients:
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced in half lengthwise or pounded thin
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder, and Italian seasoning (to taste)
  • 1–2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 sandwich rolls or ciabatta buns
  • 1/2 cup prepared basil pesto (store-bought or homemade)
  • 4 slices fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1–2 cups arugula or baby spinach

Baked Oats

    Ingredients:
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/3 cup almond milk (or milk of choice)
  • 1 medium ripe banana (or 1/2 cup applesauce)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter or coconut oil
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup (or sweetener of choice)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon (optional)
  • Optional mix-ins: nuts, berries, chocolate chips
    Instructions:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a ramekin or small baking dish with cooking spray.
  2. In a blender or mixing bowl, combine oats, almond milk, banana, egg, melted butter, maple syrup, vanilla extract, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon until smooth and well mixed.
  3. Stir in any optional mix-ins like nuts or berries.
  4. Pour the mixture into the prepared dish and smooth the top.
  5. Bake for about 25–30 minutes, or until the top is set and lightly golden.
  6. Let cool slightly before serving. Enjoy warm, optionally topped with fresh fruit, nut butter, or syrup.

Ranch Pasta Salad

    Instructions:
  1. Cook the rotini pasta in salted boiling water until soft or al dente, then drain and rinse under cold water to cool.
  2. Dice broccoli, cucumbers, and tomatoes into small, bite-sized pieces.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cooled pasta, chopped vegetables, and parmesan cheese.
  4. Pour ranch dressing over the mixture and stir well to coat everything evenly.
  5. Serve immediately or refrigerate for 1–2 hours to chill and let flavors meld.
  6. If refrigerated, stir again before serving and add more dressing if needed to moisten.
    Ingredients:
  • 12 ounces rotini pasta
  • 1 cup chopped broccoli
  • 1 cup chopped cucumbers (peeled & seeded)
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • 1¼ cups ranch dressing (store-bought or homemade)

Protein Smoothie Bowl

    Ingredients:
  • ¼ cup ice
  • ¼ cup frozen blueberries
  • ½ cup frozen strawberries
  • ½ of a small banana, frozen
  • 3 tablespoons vanilla or chocolate protein powder
  • ¾ cup unsweetened almond milk
  • Dash of salt
    Instructions:
  1. Add all ingredients to a blender and blend until smooth. Avoid adding extra almond milk to keep the consistency thick.
  2. Pour the smoothie into a bowl and add your favorite toppings such as fresh berries, sliced bananas, granola, sliced almonds, coconut, nut butter, honey, or chia seeds.
